I am excited to announce that the track previously shared on this blog as The Fate of Ophelia is officially heading to all streaming platforms!
To ensure the best possible reach and to comply with global store guidelines, the song has been retitled “The Queen of My Dreams.” The lyrics and the heart of the song remain exactly the same—a tribute to rewriting destiny and finding light in the crowded gloom. Stay tuned for the official release link!
